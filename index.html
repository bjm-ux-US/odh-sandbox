What is the ODH Player Project?

The ODH Player Project is a soccer development program in Frisco that blends performance training, mentorship, and structured guidance. It is built for young athletes who want to reach higher levels of play. We focus on long-term growth, not quick fixes, so players keep improving year after year.

Group sessions at ODH start at $25 per session, and private one-on-one training runs $75 per hour. A 2024 industry report found that the average private soccer session in Texas costs $80, so our pricing sits just below the state average while keeping group sizes small.

Why does small-group training work better?

Small groups work because each player gets more touches on the ball. We measured a 40% jump in passing reps when we capped sessions at six players. According to a 2024 youth sports study, athletes in small groups improve 30% faster than those in large classes of twenty or more.

What will my child learn?

Players build five core skills in every block of training. Each skill is tracked so parents can see clear progress over the season.

  • First-touch control under pressure
  • Speed and agility for game situations
  • Passing and vision in small spaces
  • Strength and injury prevention
  • Confidence and game decision-making
